Btown Basketball League (BBL)

A simple, low-cost ($20) basketball league built around Wednesday nights, balanced teams, and good full-court runs.

This is a relatively serious pickup league for players who already know how to hoop, so those on the sidelines stay entertained.

The goal is to make it feel like more than just one weekly game: a competitive run with standings, a prize to play for, and a groupchat that helps people organize pickup runs across Burlington between league nights.

I want a space where people can hang out, bring friends or family, and enjoy a good night around the court, bringing food and all.

Follow my Burlington newsletter: BtownBrief.com.
